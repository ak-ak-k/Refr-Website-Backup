Campaign

A campaign describes the details of the rewards & deals the business is offering to its customers. A campaign can be seen on the businesses’ store card, as well as its store page on the Refr app. The reward values mentioned on the campaign are in form of app cash where the value of one app cash is equal to 1 INR

Campaigns are set by and are under the sole discretion of the merchant
